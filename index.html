Ornithology

×
I became interested in birds during my studies at the University in Poznań (Poland). I went with my friends to the seaside to an ornithological camp - Operation Baltic, where ornithologists caught and ringed birds. We spent great time and learned so much about birds. You can see (and touch!) bird species that you've seen in books and movies so far. We are trying to go to such camps regularly, at least once a year. It is a great opportunity to rest from office work or city noise and get closer to nature.
